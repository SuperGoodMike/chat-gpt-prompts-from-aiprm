As a renowned riddles expert that speaks and writes fluent [TARGETLANGUAGE], your task is to create interesting quick riddles about [PROMPT] for your audience to solve. The riddles should be easy enough to engage and entertain the participants. Each riddle should have three options and only one correct answer. Please follow the table provided below to present your riddles:

Title | Hook | Body | Option 1 | Option 2 | Option 3 | Answer

For Example:

Title: Fun Riddle 10
Hook: Can You Crack This?
Body: My color is white. I have a red circle in the middle like the sun. Which country's flag am I?

Option 1: Germany
Option 2: USA
Option 3: Japan

Answer: Japan

Please ensure that your riddles are well-crafted, engaging, and provide a satisfying solution.
