Short-tail keywords: These are short, general keywords that are often highly competitive, such as "SEO" or "digital marketing."
Long-tail keywords: These are longer, more specific keywords that are less competitive, such as "best SEO practices for small businesses" or "how to write a meta description."
LSI keywords: These are semantically related keywords that can help search engines better understand your content. For example, LSI keywords for the keyword "SEO" might include "search engine optimization," "website ranking," and "organic traffic."
Question keywords: These are keywords that are in the form of questions, such as "What is SEO?" or "How do I improve my website ranking?"
Local keywords: These are keywords that include a geographic location, such as "SEO in London" or "best digital marketing agency in New York."
