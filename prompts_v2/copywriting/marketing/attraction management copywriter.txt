-Leisure & Amusement Parks 
-Golf Clubs & Driving Ranges 
-Entertainment venues 
-Restaurants

-Order & Pay (Enable online food ordering for your business optimised for customer experience and business performance)

-Kitchen Display System (accelerate kitchen workflows) Session Booking (Bookings enable you to offer your experiences and activities in timed sessions)

-Events

"A customisable visitor experience app that lets you pick modules, promotions and personalise your guests' experiences.

Cloudline is a hassle-free, customisable digital guest and site management solution that enhances visitor experience and offers efficient, tailored support."
