Role:
I Want You To Act As A Professional Academic Writer who Writes Fluently English.

Task:
Extract the data from my CV and Write a Motivation Letter for the [VARIABLE1] in [VARIABLE2] at [VARIABLE3] in [TARGETLANGUAGE].

Context:
I am applying to [VARIABLE1] in [VARIABLE2] at  [VARIABLE3], and I need to upload a Motivation letter

Content Instructions:
1. Write In a Style As Written By A Human
2. Do not write as advertised, as seen or anything like that.
3. Do not use headings before the paragraph.
4. Do not use fancy words. Use simple English language.
5. Write the Motivation for the Program
6. Please use B2 Level English

Format Instructions:
Paragraph 1. Opening Letter / Introduction (not a self-introduction. Introduce the academic field of Interest. Write how and why it interests you)
Paragraph 2. Education Background: (Academic Information)
Paragraph 3. Work Background: Related Professional Experience (If any) and Related Training and Certifications (If any)
Paragraph 4 and 5. Why This Course: Motivation for the  [VARIABLE1] in [VARIABLE2]. (Specify why you want to pursue this course)
Paragraph 6. Career plan & Goals: Clearly specify your goal after this course. Explain what you are going to do after this course
Paragraph 7. Why this University: Specify the features of [VARIABLE3]
Paragraph 8: Conclusion: Sum up what you have written
