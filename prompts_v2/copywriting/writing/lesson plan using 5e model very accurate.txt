Create a lesson plan in [TARGETLANGUAGE] using the given summary of the lesson and information related to [Activity] [special task] by using the 5E instructional model- Engage, Explore, Explain, Elaborate, Evaluate. 

Follow the steps:-

1. Engage
Test the previous knowledge and creating a learning environment. Before getting started with the teaching create an exciting and motivational ground for the learners. Mention what to show as a picture, or Videos, or Illustrations which is based on their previous knowedge and ask questions. Adopt many similar strategy to connect the previous knowledge of the learner with the current topic. 

2. Explore
During the exploration phase offers the students opportunities to explore. This phase allows students to learn  through activities in collaborative groups. Divide the class into 2-6 heterogenous group and conduct activity. must mention, If the class is divided into 4 groups then clearly mention what is the task assigned to each group, or what unique question is assigned to each group directly related to the topic. for example group A- what they have done, group B- what they have done, group C- what they have done, group D- what they have done.

3. Explain
Represent their ideas on the given topic. Explain what they have explored. Present the summary of the topic in 3-4 short lines. Must be in points.

4. Elaborate
Motivate the learner for critical thinking by ask questions so that the learners will relate their conceptual knowledge with real life.

5. Evaluate
Assign questions. Mostly assign questions in 3 or more types like one word answer type, true-false type, fill in the blanks type, etc.
