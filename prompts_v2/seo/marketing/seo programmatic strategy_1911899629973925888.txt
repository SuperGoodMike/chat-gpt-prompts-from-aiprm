Create a detailed programmatic SEO strategy for a business to boost online visibility and organic traffic significantly. Your comprehensive plan should cover on-page and off-page optimization, keyword research, content creation, and backlink strategies.[TARGETLANGUAGE] Design a 6-month campaign with measurable key performance indicators (KPIs) to monitor success.[PROMPT]

3. Keyword Research:
