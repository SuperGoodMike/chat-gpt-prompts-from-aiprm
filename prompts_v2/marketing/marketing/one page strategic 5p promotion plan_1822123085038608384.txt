Help me create a 5P Promotion Plan. It's a clear and succinct method for developing and executing a marketing strategy.

I'd like to train you on how to become a 5P Promotion Plan expert.

Here is an example of a 5P Promotion plan:

Every successful promotion plan clearly answers the following: 

1. Who is the prospect?
2. What is the painkiller problem they have?
3. What is the product to solve that problem?
4. What proof can we show that we have solved that problem for others?
5. What is the pathway to promote this product

Example:
1. Prospect: My ideal Client is an established consultant with good intellectual property that is active on LinkedIn and has 500+ connections and the average value of a client is $10K+ based in UK, Australia, New Zealand, USA.


- Are consultants and professional advisors with 10+ years experience
- Work with high-value clients (worth $10K+ in annually)
- Have valuable Intellectual Property to share
- Have existing clients that know, like and trust them
- $250K+ annual revenues (or the capacity to exceed $20K per month)
- Have a strong personal & professional network,
- Have an existing word of mouth & referrals
- Have an internal resource to help implement or can devote 3 hours per week.
- Are friendly & coachable,
- Have a very strong desire to scale via digital marketing

2. Problem: Lead generation is a problem Entrepreneurs & sales heads are wasting upto 3 hours a day prospecting. They don’t know how to quickly and profitably use LinkedIn to generate qualified leads.

3. Promise: The Winning LinkedIn Formula (WLF) is a 5 Day program that will generate 5-7 qualified appointments per week through LinkedIn in just 15 minutes a day.

4. Product: The five day course shows each entrepreneur how to design a powerfully optimised Linkedin profile, position themselves as an expert, prospect Linkedin with ninja skills & profitably Turn Contacts into Contracts for maximum impact & income. 

5. Proof: The program is being run by Neeraj Shah a leading digital marketing and Linkedin Expert on GeniusU the world’s No. 1 Entrepreneur Education Platform with over 1.5 million entrepreneurs. 

5. Pathway: 

1. Lead Magnet > Challenge > Call > Microdegree or 1-1 Coaching
2. Webinar>Lead Magnet> Call>1-1 Coaching or Microdegree
3. Chat bot Quiz>Lead Magnet>Call>Course or 1-1 Coaching
4. LinkedIn Post for Lead Magent>Lead Magnet>Call>Course or Coaching
5. Email List> Viral Lead Magnet> Challenge > Course or Coaching

6. Purpose: WLF is part of our mission to inspire and educate entrepreneurs to elevate their purpose, passion & profits to make the world a better place. Aligned with 17 UN Global Goals - Quality Education
