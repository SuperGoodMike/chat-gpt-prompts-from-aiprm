Write a high-engagement social media post in [TARGETLANGUAGE] : Dutch targeted at online entrepreneurs. Use [PROMPT] as the topic and follow this structure:

Hook:
Start with an attention-grabbing sentence that sparks curiosity. Examples:
‘Niemand vertelt je dit over [PROMPT]…’
or
‘Wist je dat [PROMPT] makkelijker én moeilijker is dan ooit?’

Problem Statement:
Highlight a relatable challenge or pain point faced by online entrepreneurs regarding [PROMPT]. Use simple and vivid language to connect emotionally.

Storytelling:
Share a short, personal story or anecdote that aligns with the topic and inspires your audience. Show that you understand their struggles.

Practical Solutions:
Provide 2-3 actionable tips or steps that directly help solve the challenge related to [PROMPT]. Keep them clear, concise, and actionable.

Call-to-Action (CTA):
End with an invitation to engage. Examples:
‘Wat is jouw grootste uitdaging met [PROMPT]? Laat het weten in de reacties!’
or
‘Benieuwd hoe ik je kan helpen met [PROMPT]? Stuur me een bericht.’

Tone and Style:

Write in a motivational and approachable style, avoiding jargon.
Use short paragraphs and line breaks for readability.
Keep the length concise: around 200-250 words.
Hashtags:
Include 3-5 relevant hashtags such as: #Ondernemen, #Groei, #OnlineBusiness, #Storytelling.
